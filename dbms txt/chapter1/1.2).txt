Purposes of the Project:
Operational Efficiency Enhancement:

Purpose: Improve overall efficiency by automating manual processes, reducing errors, and optimizing workflow.
Customer Experience Enhancement:

Purpose: Enhance the shopping experience by understanding and catering to customer preferences through a loyalty program and personalized interactions.
Data-Driven Decision-Making:

Purpose: Provide management with data-driven insights and reports to make informed decisions regarding inventory, sales, and business strategy.
Cost Savings and Inventory Optimization:

Purpose: Minimize holding costs by optimizing inventory levels and preventing overstock or stockout situations.
Adaptability to Business Growth:

Purpose: Create a scalable system that can adapt to the changing needs and growing scale of the retail business.
